Our Origin Story

Fueled by a deep love for all things spicy, Chef Jones started his pepper sauce empire right in his home kitchen. What began as a personal quest to create the perfect fiery concoction soon exploded into a local sensation. His distinctive sauces, bursting with flavor and just the right amount of heat, quickly gained a loyal following at local events and BBQs.

The word spread, and soon, the buzz around JoJo's meant Chef Jones needed a way to bring his flavorful creations to even more of you. That's why he's thrilled to now offer his beloved sauces directly to your door.
